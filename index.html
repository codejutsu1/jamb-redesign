The JAMB Mandate

"JAMB: Empowering Education, Enabling Success."

"The mandate of the Joint Admissions and Matriculation Board, established by Act No. 2 of 1978 and amended by Decree No. 33 of 1989, empowers the Board to conduct Matriculation Examinations, appoint personnel, facilitate admissions to tertiary institutions based on guidelines and preferences, collate and disseminate admission-related information, and carry out necessary activities for the fulfillment of its functions
